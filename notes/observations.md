# Running Observations

## General Patterns (updating as I go)

### Dialogue vs. narration
- Hypothesis: dialogue-heavy passages with social register information will show higher cultural failure rates than descriptive narration
- Reason: dialogue is where keigo, gendered speech, and social dynamics are encoded most densely

### System differences
- Tracking whether GPT-4, Claude, Google Translate, and TranslateGemma fail differently
- Initial impression: GPT-4 and Claude sometimes attempt cultural adaptation but inconsistently; Google Translate and TranslateGemma are more literal

### Metric blindness
- Need to test: do COMET and BERTScore rank culturally flat translations similarly to culturally adapted ones?
- If yes, this is evidence for a gap in current evaluation
